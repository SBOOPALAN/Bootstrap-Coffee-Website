About Us

What Makes Our coffee Special

Cafe Patachou has such a friendly, congenial vibe that it has long been called a "student union for adults." Owned by Martha Hoover, a four-time

James Beard Award semifinalist for outstanding restaurateur, it has also been named among the nation's Top 10 breakfast spots by Bon Apetit. Its

website design is sunny, colorful and full of food photography that captures the premium scratch cooking on its menu. The website also features a robust

merchandise store that sells apparel, mugs, coffee and the restaurant's signature cinnamon-sugar blend.
